# Restricted data notice

The raw mobile phone signalling data used in this study were obtained from a third-party mobile network operator under a data licensing agreement for academic research. These data were anonymized and aggregated before analysis, but the raw records cannot be redistributed because of privacy and licensing restrictions.

The raw corporate registration linkage records were obtained from third-party corporate registration databases. These raw records cannot be redistributed because of database licensing restrictions.

This repository therefore provides:

1. analysis code;
2. data schemas;
3. sample input files;
4. derived aggregate indices;
5. figure source data;
6. documentation sufficient to understand the computational workflow.

Access to restricted data may be requested from the corresponding author, subject to approval by the data providers, applicable data-use agreements and legal/privacy requirements.
